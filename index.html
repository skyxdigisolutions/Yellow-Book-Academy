About Us

Welcome to Yellow Book Academy

Yellow Book Academy, founded by Mrs. Suvarna T Prabhu, (M.Com,MBA) Who has a 12 years of experience in Accounts,Taxation,Finance and Management. It is an innovative online e-learning platform dedicated to UG, PG, and PUC students. The academy offers a comprehensive range of courses designed to enhance academic performance and provide students with the tools they need to succeed in their studies. With a focus on quality education and personalized learning experiences, Yellow Book Academy is committed to empowering students to achieve their academic goals from the comfort of their own homes.
